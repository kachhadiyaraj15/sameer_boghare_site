# Surat Mayor Fund - Required Documents

## Documents Required

1. Certified copy of barcoded ration card (Must be from Surat city area)
2. Certified copy of the applicant's Voter Card (Election ID) and Aadhaar Card
3. Certified copy of the patient's Voter Card (Election ID) and Aadhaar Card
4. For newborn: Birth Certificate, Parents' Aadhaar Card and Voter Card (certified copies)
5. For minor patient: Certified copy of Aadhaar Card
6. In case of patient's death: Certified copy of Death Certificate
7. Certified copy of last Property Tax Bill of the address mentioned in application
8. Income Certificate from Mamlatdar/Competent Authority regarding annual family income
9. Certified copy of applicant's Bank Passbook first page / Cancelled Cheque
10. Original final bill signed and stamped by hospital doctor + Payment receipt + Original medicine bills with prescription
11. Application form with hospital signature
12. Summary of all treatment bills with total cost on separate sheet
13. Original Discharge Certificate signed by hospital doctor
14. Maa Amritam Card / Ayushman Card copy (if available, else submit Ayushman Card)

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Please Note:**

- Applicant must be a resident of Surat City area only.
- All documents must be certified copies.
- Bills must be arranged in order with a summary sheet showing total cost.
